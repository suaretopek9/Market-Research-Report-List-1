<p><p>A vacuum evaporation boat is a small vessel typically made of materials like tungsten, molybdenum, or graphite, used in vacuum deposition processes. These boats are used to hold and heat the material being evaporated in order to create a thin film on a substrate. Vacuum evaporation boats are widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, aerospace, and medical for applications like coatings, semiconductor manufacturing, and solar cell production.</p><p>The Vacuum Evaporation Boat Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
