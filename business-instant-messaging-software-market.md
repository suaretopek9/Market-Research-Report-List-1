<p><p>The business instant messaging software market can be divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and managed on the company's own servers and infrastructure, giving them full control and customization options but requiring high upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted and managed by a third-party provider, allowing businesses to access it via the internet. This option offers lower upfront costs, scalability, and hassle-free maintenance, but businesses have less control over customization and data security.</p></p>
