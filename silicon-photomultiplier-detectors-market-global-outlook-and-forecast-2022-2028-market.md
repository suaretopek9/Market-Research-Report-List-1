The market can be categorized into two types: Analog Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) and Digital Silicon Photomultiplier (dSiPM). The analog SiPM detectors provide a continuous output signal proportional to the incident light intensity, while the digital SiPM detectors convert the incident light into digital signals, allowing for more precise and accurate measurements.
