<p><p>Cappuccino makers come in four main market types: Manual, Semi-Automatic, Fully Automatic, and Super-Automatic. Manual machines require the user to control the entire brewing process. Semi-Automatic machines automate the brewing process but still require manual control over milk frothing. Fully Automatic machines handle both espresso extraction and milk frothing with the push of a button. Super-Automatic machines are the most advanced, handling everything from grinding beans to pouring the final cappuccino without any manual intervention.</p></p>
