<p><p>Copper foil shielding tape is available in two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided tape has adhesive on only one side, making it ideal for applications where only one side of the surface needs to be shielded. Double-sided tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on both sides, providing a stronger bond and better shielding effectiveness. Both types of tape are commonly used in electronic devices, cables, and equipment to protect against electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
