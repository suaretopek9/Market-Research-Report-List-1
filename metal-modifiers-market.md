<p><strong>The Metal Modifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Metal Modifiers</li><li>Inorganic Metal Modifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal modifiers are substances added to metal alloys to improve their mechanical properties. There are two types of metal modifiers: organic and inorganic. Organic metal modifiers consist of carbon-based compounds that enhance the alloy's strength and durability. Inorganic metal modifiers, on the other hand, are made up of non-carbon-based compounds such as oxides, sulfides, or nitrides that also improve the alloy's properties. Both types of metal modifiers play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of metal alloys in various industrial applications.</p></p>
