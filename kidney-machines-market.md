<p><p>Kidney Machines refer to medical devices used to perform blood purification in patients with kidney failure. The market for kidney machines can be categorized into different types. Continuous Blood Purification (CBP) is a method that continuously filters the blood to remove waste products, while Hemodialysis (HD) involves periodically filtering the blood using an artificial kidney machine. Other markets within the kidney machines market may include home-based dialysis machines or wearable devices. These different types cater to the specific needs and preferences of patients, providing options for effective kidney support and improving their quality of life.</p></p>
