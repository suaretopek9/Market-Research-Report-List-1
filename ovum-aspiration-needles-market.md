<p><p>Ovum aspiration needles are used in fertility treatments to extract oocytes (eggs) from a woman's ovaries. The market for these needles can be categorized into two types: single lumen and double-lumen needles. Single lumen needles have one channel through which the aspiration process takes place, while double-lumen needles have two channels. The main difference is that double-lumen needles allow for simultaneous flushing and aspiration, which can improve the efficiency of the procedure. Both types of needles are essential in assisted reproductive technology to help individuals or couples conceive.</p></p>
