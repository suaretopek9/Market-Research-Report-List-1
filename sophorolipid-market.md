<p><p>Sophorolipids are a type of microbial surfactants that are categorized into two main types based on their chemical structure and properties: lactonic sophorolipids and acidic sophorolipids. Lactonic sophorolipids have a lactone ring structure which gives them unique surface-active properties, while acidic sophorolipids have carboxylic acid groups that contribute to their emulsifying capabilities. Both types of sophorolipids have diverse applications in various industries, including personal care, cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
