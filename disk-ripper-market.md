<p><ul><li>Single Action</li><li>Offset Type</li><li>Double Action</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disk rippers are agricultural implements used for deep tilling the soil. The Single Action disk ripper has a single row of ripper shanks, the Offset Type has ripper shanks staggered to one side for better coverage, and the Double Action features ripper shanks on both sides for maximum soil disruption. Each type offers unique benefits for farmers depending on the soil condition and desired tillage depth. Farmers can choose the appropriate disk ripper type based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
