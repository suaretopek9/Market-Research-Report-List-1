<p><p>The minimally invasive surgical handheld instruments market is classified into different types. Graspers are used to hold, manipulate, and probe the tissues during surgery. Retractors/Elevators are employed to keep the surgical area open and create space for the surgeon's access and visibility. Suturing instruments aid in stitching tissues together. Dilators help enlarge anatomical passages or structures. Lastly, there are various other instruments, such as scissors, forceps, and needles, used for specific purposes in minimally invasive surgeries. These instruments play crucial roles in ensuring accurate and precise surgical procedures while minimizing patient trauma.</p></p>
