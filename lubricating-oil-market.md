<p><p>The Lubricating Oil market can be classified into four main types based on their physical state- Liquid, Solid, Semi-liquid, and Gaseous. Liquid lubricating oils are the most commonly used and versatile form, providing lubrication in a wide range of applications. Solid lubricants offer high resistance to extreme conditions and are often used in heavy-duty machinery. Semi-liquid lubes combine the properties of both liquid and solid lubes. Gaseous lubricants are primarily used in specialized industrial applications where traditional forms are not suitable.</p></p>
