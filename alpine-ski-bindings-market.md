<p><ul><li>Frame Bindings</li><li>Tech Bindings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alpine ski bindings come in two main types: frame bindings and tech bindings. Frame bindings are a traditional type where the toe and heel pieces are connected by a frame that sits on top of the ski. They are known for their durability and ease of use. On the other hand, tech bindings are a newer, lighter weight option that uses pins to attach the boot to the ski. They are popular among backcountry skiers for their tourability and efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and cater to different ski styles and preferences.</p></p>
