<p><p>A wire type spirometer is a medical device used to measure lung capacity and the flow of air into and out of the lungs. It consists of a chamber that captures exhaled breath, a pressure transducer that converts the pressure change inside the chamber into electrical signals, and a wire that detects these signals.</p><p>The wire type spirometer market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), and lung cancer. According to the Global Burden of Disease Study, these diseases accounted for over 8 million deaths worldwide in 2017. Hence, the demand for spirometers to diagnose and monitor these conditions is growing rapidly.</p><p>Moreover, the aging population and the rise in smoking habits are further driving the market growth.
