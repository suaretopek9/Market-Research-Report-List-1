<p><strong>The Quantum Dot (QD) Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QDEF</li><li>QLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum Dot (QD) Display technology is utilized in two main market types: QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) and QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode). QDEF involves placing a thin layer of Quantum Dots on top of a standard LCD panel to enhance color reproduction and brightness. On the other hand, QLED uses Quantum Dots as the light source in a display, resulting in deeper blacks, higher contrast ratios, and improved energy efficiency compared to traditional LED displays. Both technologies offer superior image quality and are gaining popularity in the display market.</p></p>
