<p><p>Inconel Alloy Flange market offers a variety of types to suit different industrial requirements. Integral Flanges are forged as part of the fitting, ensuring high strength and reliability. Threaded Flanges are designed for easy installation by screwing onto pipes. Butt Welding Flanges are welded to the pipe for a secure connection. Loose Flanges allow for easy dismantling and maintenance. Other types include socket weld, lap joint, and blind flanges, each offering specific advantages for different applications.</p></p>
