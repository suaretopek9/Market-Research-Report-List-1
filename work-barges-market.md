<p><p>Work barges come in two main market types: monohull and multihull. Monohull work barges have a single hull design and are typically more stable in rough waters. They are ideal for transporting heavy loads over long distances. Multihull work barges have multiple hulls, providing increased stability and buoyancy. They are often used for specialized tasks such as diving operations or offshore construction. Both monohull and multihull work barges play important roles in industries such as construction, dredging, and oil and gas.</p></p>
