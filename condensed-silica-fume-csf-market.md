<p><p>Condensed Silica Fume (CSF) is classified into three types based on its density: Densified Silica Fume, Semi Densified Silica Fume, and Undensified Silica Fume. Densified Silica Fume has the highest density and is most commonly used in construction applications requiring high strength and durability. Semi Densified Silica Fume falls in the middle range of density and is used in a variety of construction projects. Undensified Silica Fume has the lowest density and is typically used in applications where flowability and ease of handling are important.</p></p>
