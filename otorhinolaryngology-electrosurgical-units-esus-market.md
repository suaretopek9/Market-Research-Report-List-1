<p><p>Otorhinolaryngology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) encompass various types including Monopole Electrosurgical Units, Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, and Vessel Sealing Units. Monopole ESUs are commonly used for cutting and coagulating tissues with a single active electrode. Bipolar ESUs are used for delicate surgeries as they utilize two closely spaced electrodes to minimize tissue damage. Vessel Sealing Units are specifically designed for sealing blood vessels during surgeries to minimize bleeding. Each type of ESU serves a specific purpose in Otorhinolaryngology procedures.</p></p>
