<p><strong>The Nuclear Grade Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixture Nuclear Grade Resins</li><li>Nuclear Grade Anion Resins</li><li>Nuclear Grade Cationic Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear Grade Resins refer to specialized resins used in nuclear power plants for water purification and treatment. These resins are divided into three main types: Mixture Nuclear Grade Resins, which are a combination of anion and cationic resins for enhanced purification; Nuclear Grade Anion Resins, which remove anions like nitrate and sulfate from water; and Nuclear Grade Cationic Resins, which remove cations like sodium and calcium. Each type of resin plays a crucial role in maintaining the quality of water used in nuclear power plants.</p></p>
