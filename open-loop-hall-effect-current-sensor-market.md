<p><p>Open-loop Hall-Effect current sensors can be classified into two main market types based on their output characteristics. Linear output sensors provide a continuous, proportional output signal that directly correlates with the measured current. On the other hand, threshold output sensors provide a binary output signal that switches on or off based on a set current threshold. Both types of sensors are widely used in various applications such as industrial control systems, automotive electronics, and power management systems.</p></p>
