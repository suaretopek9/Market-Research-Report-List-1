<p><p>Natural chitosan is a biopolymer derived from chitin, a naturally occurring polysaccharide found in the exoskeletons of crustaceans such as shrimp, crabs, and lobsters. It possesses unique properties such as biocompatibility, biodegradability, non-toxicity, and high adsorption capabilities, making it widely used in various industries including food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, agriculture, and wastewater treatment.</p><p>The natural chitosan market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for natural and sustainable products, coupled with growing awareness about the harmful effects of synthetic chemicals, is driving the market growth. Natural chitosan offers eco-friendly solutions and has a wide range of applications, which is further fueling its adoption.</p><p>The food and beverage industry is one of the major sectors driving the growth of the natural chitosan market. It is used as a food preservative, thickening agent, and antimicrobial agent in various food products. The rising demand for natural food preservatives and the need to extend the shelf life of perishable food products are contributing to the market growth.</p><p>In the pharmaceutical industry, natural chitosan is utilized in drug delivery systems, wound healing, tissue engineering, and as an antimicrobial agent. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, rising healthcare expenditure, and advancements in drug delivery systems are driving the demand for natural chitosan in the pharmaceutical sector.</p><p>In the cosmetics industry, natural chitosan is used in skincare products, hair care products, and as a film-forming agent. The growing consumer preference for natural and organic personal care products is propelling the market growth.</p><p>In the agriculture sector, natural chitosan is used as a biopesticide, plant growth enhancer, and fertilizer. The rising demand for organic farming practices and the need for sustainable agricultural solutions are boosting the adoption of natural chitosan.</p><p>Overall, the natural chitosan market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.2% during the forecast period.
