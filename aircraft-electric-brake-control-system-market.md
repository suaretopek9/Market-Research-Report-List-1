<p><p>The Aircraft Electric Brake Control System market consists of different types of brakes, including single disc brakes, dual-disc brakes, and multiple-disc brakes. Single disc brakes are designed with a single brake disc and are widely used in smaller aircraft. Dual-disc brakes feature two brake discs, offering enhanced braking performance and heat dissipation for medium-sized aircraft. Multiple-disc brakes, with three or more brake discs, provide the highest level of braking power and are typically found in large and heavy aircraft. These different types cater to varying aircraft sizes and braking requirements.</p></p>
