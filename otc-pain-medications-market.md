<p><p>OTC pain medications, also known as over-the-counter pain medications, are drugs that can be purchased without a prescription for relieving pain. Additionally, the convenience and accessibility of OTC pain medications, coupled with their affordability compared to prescription drugs, further contribute to market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the OTC pain medications market is the increasing demand for natural and herbal pain relief products.
