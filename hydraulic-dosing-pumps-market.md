<p><p>Hydraulic dosing pumps are classified into two main types: diaphragm pumps and piston pumps. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create suction and discharge pressure, making them suitable for accurate dosing of fluids in various industrial applications. On the other hand, piston pumps utilize a reciprocating piston to displace and meter fluid, offering high pressure and flow rate capabilities. Both types of pumps are widely used in industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, and oil and gas for precise and efficient dosing of chemicals and additives.</p></p>
