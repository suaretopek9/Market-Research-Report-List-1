<p><p>Modified asphalt is categorized under two market types: Thermoplastic elastomers and plastomers. Thermoplastic elastomers are a type of polymer material that exhibits both thermoplastic and elastomeric properties, allowing for flexibility and durability in asphalt applications. Plastomers are also polymer materials with a unique structure that combines characteristics of both plastics and elastomers. Both market types offer enhanced performance and durability compared to traditional asphalt formulations, making them popular choices in the construction and infrastructure industries.</p></p>
