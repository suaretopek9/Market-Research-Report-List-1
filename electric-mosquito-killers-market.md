<p><p>The electric mosquito killers market includes three main types: electronic mosquito killers, sticky mosquito killers, and air flow suction mosquito killers. Electronic mosquito killers use UV light to attract and electrocute mosquitoes. Sticky mosquito killers use adhesive surfaces to trap mosquitoes. Air flow suction mosquito killers use a fan to create airflow that sucks mosquitoes into a sealed container. Each type offers unique methods of effectively eliminating mosquitoes, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers seeking relief from these pesky insects.</p></p>
