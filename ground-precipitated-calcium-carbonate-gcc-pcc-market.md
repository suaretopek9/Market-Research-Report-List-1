<p><p>Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC) are both forms of calcium carbonate used in various industries such as paper, paint, plastics, and pharmaceuticals. GCC is obtained by grinding natural limestone rock into a fine powder, while PCC is produced through a chemical process using limestone as a raw material. GCC is more cost-effective and widely used, while PCC offers superior quality and higher brightness. Both products play a crucial role in various applications due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
