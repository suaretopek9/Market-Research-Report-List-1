<p><p>Veneer-faced panels for furniture are available in two main types: particleboard faced and MDF faced. Particleboard faced panels are made of compressed wood particles covered with a thin layer of veneer. They are cost-effective and commonly used for budget-friendly furniture. On the other hand, MDF faced panels are made of medium-density fiberboard and offer a smoother surface for a more premium finish. They are popular for high-end furniture pieces due to their durability and versatility in design options.</p></p>
