<p><p>Photocuring agents are widely used in various industries for curing adhesives, coatings, and inks. There are two main types of photocuring agents - free-radical type and cationic type. Free-radical type agents initiate polymerization through the generation of free radicals, while cationic type agents initiate polymerization through the generation of cations. Both types have their own unique properties and applications, making them suitable for different types of materials and curing processes. The photocuring agents market continues to grow as industries seek more efficient and environmentally friendly curing methods.</p></p>
