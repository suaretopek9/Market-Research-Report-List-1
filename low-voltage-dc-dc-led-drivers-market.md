<p><p>Low voltage DC-DC LED drivers are categorized into different types based on their functionality and design. The Buck driver steps down the voltage to provide a constant current output to the LED, while the Boost driver steps up the voltage. Multi-channel drivers are capable of driving multiple LEDs simultaneously. Other types of drivers include dimmable drivers, constant current drivers, and programmable drivers. Each type offers unique features and benefits to meet the specific requirements of LED lighting applications.</p></p>
