<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforcements market types include Roving, Chopped Strand, Chopped Strand Mat (CSM), Continuous Filament Mat (CFM), Fabrics, and Other. Roving is continuous strands of fibers used for filament winding and weaving. Chopped Strand is short fibers used in thermoplastics and BMC. CSM consists of randomly oriented chopped strands bonded together with a binder for molding processes. CFM is parallel layers of continuous fibers for reinforcement. Fabrics are woven or knitted structures used in composites. Other types may include non-woven mats or stitched reinforcements.</p></p>
