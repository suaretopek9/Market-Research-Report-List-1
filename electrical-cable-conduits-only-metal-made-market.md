<p><p>Electrical cable conduits made of metal come in two main types: flexible conduits and rigid conduits. Flexible conduits are often made of stainless steel or aluminum and are used in situations where bends or movement of the conduit is required. Rigid conduits, on the other hand, are typically made of galvanized steel and provide a more permanent and secure protection for cables. Both types play a crucial role in safely housing and protecting electrical cables in various applications.</p></p>
