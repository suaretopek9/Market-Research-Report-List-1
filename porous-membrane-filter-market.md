<p><strong>The Porous Membrane Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Porous membrane filters are classified into different types based on the material used for filtration. The inorganic membrane filter is made of ceramic materials, such as alumina or titanium dioxide, and offers high chemical resistance. PVDF filters are made of polyvinylidene fluoride, providing excellent compatibility with a wide range of solvents. PTFE filters are made of polytetrafluoroethylene, known for their hydrophobic properties. PES filters are made of polyethersulfone, offering high flow rates and low protein binding. Other types of porous membrane filters include nylon and cellulose acetate.</p></p>
