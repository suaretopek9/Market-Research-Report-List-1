<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Boronate Affinity Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bench-top HbA1c analyzers are used for measuring glycated hemoglobin levels in diabetic patients. Three main types of technologies used in these analyzers are ion exchange chromatography, immunoassay, and boronate affinity technology. Ion exchange chromatography separates HbA1c from other hemoglobin variants based on their charge. Immunoassay utilizes antibodies to specifically detect HbA1c. Boronate affinity technology binds to the sugar moiety of HbA1c for measurement. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and ease of use.</p></p>
