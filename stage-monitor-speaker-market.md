<p><strong>The Stage Monitor Speaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Monitor Speaker</li><li>Passive Monitor Speaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active monitor speakers have built-in power amplifiers, making them independent and easier to set up. They are commonly used by musicians and sound engineers for live performances or in recording studios. Passive monitor speakers require an external power amplifier to function, providing more flexibility in terms of power and sound customization. Both types cater to different needs and budgets in the market, with active speakers being more popular due to their convenience and all-in-one design, while passive speakers offer more control and customizable options.</p></p>
