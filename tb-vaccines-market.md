<p><p>TB Vaccines market can be categorized into three types: immunotherapeutic vaccines, booster vaccines, and others. Immunotherapeutic vaccines are developed to stimulate the immune system to fight against tuberculosis by targeting specific antigens. Booster vaccines are administered to individuals who have already been vaccinated in order to enhance their immune response and provide long-lasting protection against TB. The category of "others" includes different types of TB vaccines currently under development or in clinical trials, such as whole cell vaccines, recombinant vaccines, and DNA vaccines.</p></p>
