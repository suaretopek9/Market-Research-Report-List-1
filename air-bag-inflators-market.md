<p><p>Air bag inflators are crucial components in automotive safety systems. There are three main types of air bag inflators: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators use a small explosive charge to rapidly inflate the air bag, stored gas inflators rely on compressed gas to inflate the air bag, and hybrid inflators combine elements of both pyrotechnic and stored gas systems. Each type offers unique benefits and drawbacks, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
