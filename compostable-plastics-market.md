<p><p>Compostable plastics market is segmented into various types such as Polylactic Acid (PLA), Polyhydroxy Fatty Acid (PHA), Thermoplastic Starch, Polybutylene Succinate (PBS), and Others. PLA is derived from renewable resources like corn starch and is biodegradable. PHA is made from microorganisms and is fully compostable. Thermoplastic starch is a blend of starch and biodegradable polymers. PBS is a biodegradable polyester. Other types of compostable plastics include Polycaprolactone (PCL) and Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA), offering sustainable alternatives to traditional plastics.</p></p>
