<p><p>The EVA-based Solar Encapsulants market consists of two main types: EVA Film and EVA Sheet. EVA Film is a flexible, thin layer used to encapsulate solar cells in photovoltaic modules, providing protection from environmental factors. EVA Sheet, on the other hand, is a thicker, more rigid material commonly used for larger solar modules. Both EVA Film and EVA Sheet play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of solar panels by providing insulation, adhesion, and durability.</p></p>
