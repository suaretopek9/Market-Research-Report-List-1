<p><p>The military aircraft floor-standing weighing equipment market can be categorized into two types: digital and analog weighing equipment. Digital weighing equipment uses electronic sensors and displays weight measurements digitally, providing accurate and precise readings. On the other hand, analog weighing equipment utilizes mechanical components like springs and levers to measure weight, with readings shown on a physical dial or scale. Both types serve the purpose of weighing military aircrafts but differ in terms of technology and presentation of weight measurements.</p></p>
