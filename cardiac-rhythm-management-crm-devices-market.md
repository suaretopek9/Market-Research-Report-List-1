Pacemakers are implanted to help control slow heart rhythms, while Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are used to treat fast or irregular heart rhythms. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices are used for patients with heart failure to help synchronize the heart's pumping chambers. These devices play a crucial role in managing and treating various heart rhythm disorders.</p></p>
