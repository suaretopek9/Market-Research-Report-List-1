<p><ul><li>External Degaussing System</li><li>Shipborne Degaussing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vessel Degaussing System market consists of two main types: External Degaussing System and Shipborne Degaussing System. External Degaussing System is used for large vessels and involves the installation of coils around the hull to create a magnetic field that counteracts the ship's own magnetism. Shipborne Degaussing System is designed for smaller vessels and involves the use of portable degaussing coils that are placed on board the ship to neutralize its magnetic signature. Both systems are crucial for reducing the risk of magnetic mines and improving naval vessel safety.</p></p>
