<p><p>Fluorite pellets are categorized into two main types: Acid Grade Fluorite Pellets and Metallurgical Grade Fluorite Pellets. Acid Grade Fluorite Pellets are used in the production of hydrofluoric acid and high-quality ceramic and optical glass. On the other hand, Metallurgical Grade Fluorite Pellets are mainly utilized in the steelmaking process as a flux to lower the melting point of raw materials and improve the efficiency of the steel production. Both types of Fluorite pellets serve different industrial purposes based on their chemical compositions and properties.</p></p>
