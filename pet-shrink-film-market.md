<p><p>PET Shrink Film is categorized into three types based on the level of shrinkage it offers. Low shrink film provides minimal shrinkage, suitable for packaging products with irregular shapes. Medium shrink film offers moderate shrinkage, ideal for packaging products with standard shapes. High shrink film provides the most amount of shrinkage, suitable for tightly wrapping products with regular shapes. Each type of shrink film caters to different packaging needs, offering varying levels of protection and presentation for the packaged products.</p></p>
