<p><p>Diatomite is a naturally occurring, soft, sedimentary rock that is composed of the fossilized remains of diatoms. The market types for diatomite include anhydrous substance and flux calcined. Anhydrous substance diatomite is used in filtration and as an absorbent due to its high porosity and absorption qualities. Flux calcined diatomite has been treated at high temperatures to alter its chemical and physical properties, making it suitable for use in various industrial applications such as insulation, ceramics, and construction.</p></p>
