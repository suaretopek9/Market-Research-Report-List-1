<p><strong>The Automatic Platform Screen Door Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Closed Type</li><li>Semi-Closed Type</li><li>Half Height Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Platform Screen Door market consists of three types: Full-Closed Type, Semi-Closed Type, and Half Height Type. The Full-Closed Type refers to platform doors that completely enclose the platform area, providing maximum safety and protection to passengers. Semi-Closed Type platform doors are partially enclosed, allowing for natural ventilation while still safeguarding passengers. Half Height Type doors are only half the height of the platform, providing necessary safety measures without completely enclosing the platform area. These different types cater to varying safety requirements, ventilation needs, and architectural designs in different transportation systems.</p></p>
