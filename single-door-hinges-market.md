<p><p>Single door hinges are available in various types based on their design and functionality. Barrel hinges are cylindrical in shape and provide smooth rotation for the door. Butt hinges are commonly used for interior doors and provide simple functionality. Pivot hinges allow the door to pivot in both directions. HL hinges are used for heavy doors and provide strong support. Concealed hinges offer a sleek appearance as they are not visible when the door is closed. Flag hinges are ideal for flush-mount doors. There are also other types of hinges available in the market to suit different door styles and requirements.</p></p>
