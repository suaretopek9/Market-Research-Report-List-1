<p><ul><li>None-Adhesive Film</li><li>Self-Adhesive Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switchable privacy film is available in two main market types: Non-adhesive film and self-adhesive film. Non-adhesive film uses static cling technology to adhere to glass surfaces without the need for any glue, making it easy to install and remove. On the other hand, self-adhesive film has a sticky backing that allows for a more permanent application. Both types offer the benefit of privacy on demand, as the film can switch from transparent to opaque with the flip of a switch or a remote control.</p></p>
