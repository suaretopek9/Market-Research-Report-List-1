<p><strong>The Functional Adhesive Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Melt Adhesive Film</li><li>Pressure Sensitive Adhesive Film</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional adhesive films are available in various types, including hot melt adhesive film, pressure sensitive adhesive film, and others. Hot melt adhesive films are solid at room temperature but melt when heated, providing a strong bond when cooled. Pressure sensitive adhesive films adhere to surfaces upon application of slight pressure. Other types of adhesive films include heat-activated films and UV-cured films. These adhesive films serve different purposes based on their unique properties and application methods.</p></p>
