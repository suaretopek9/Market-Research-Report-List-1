<p><p>Automated immunoanalyzers are devices used in medical laboratories to accurately measure the concentration of specific molecules in biological samples. These analyzers can be classified into two types: semi-automated and fully automated. </p><p>Semi-automated immunoanalyzers require some manual steps by laboratory technicians, such as sample preparation or loading. They are suitable for laboratories with lower sample volumes or specialized testing requirements. </p><p>On the other hand, fully automated immunoanalyzers do not require any manual intervention, as they can automatically perform all necessary steps from sample processing to result interpretation. They are ideal for high-throughput laboratories that handle a large number of samples.</p></p>
