<p><p>Metallurgical limestone market includes various types such as dolomitic limestone, calcitic dolomite, and magnesian limestones. Dolomitic limestone contains a high percentage of magnesium carbonate, while calcitic dolomite has a higher ratio of calcium carbonate to magnesium carbonate. Magnesian limestones are characterized by their high magnesium content. Each type of limestone has different chemical compositions and properties, making them suitable for specific industrial applications in metallurgical processes. Companies in the metallurgical limestone market may choose to offer one or more of these types based on customer needs and market demand.</p></p>
