<p><ul><li>Distance Connection</li><li>Variable Distance Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile scaffolding refers to a portable framework used to support workers and materials during construction or maintenance tasks. Distance connection and variable distance connection are two types of scaffolding in the market. Distance connection refers to scaffolding systems where the frames are connected to each other with fixed intervals, ensuring a consistent gap between horizontal cross braces. On the other hand, variable distance connection scaffolding allows users to adjust the gap between frames according to specific project requirements, providing flexibility in terms of functionality and adaptability to different work environments.</p></p>
