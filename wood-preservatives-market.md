<p><p>Wood preservatives are products used to protect wood from decay, rot, and insect damage. There are three main types of wood preservatives in the market: water-based, solvent-based, and oil-based. Water-based preservatives are commonly used for indoor applications due to their low toxicity and environmental friendliness. Solvent-based preservatives are effective for outdoor use and provide long-lasting protection against moisture. Oil-based preservatives are ideal for enhancing the natural beauty of wood while protecting it from weathering and pests. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific application.</p></p>
