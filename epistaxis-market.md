<p><p>Epistaxis, also known as nosebleeds, has several market types aimed at treating and addressing the condition. Antibiotic ointments prevent infection and promote healing. Cauterizing agents are used to burn or destroy tissue, allowing for the sealing of blood vessels and stopping the bleeding.
