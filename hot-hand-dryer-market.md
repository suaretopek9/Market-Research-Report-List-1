<p><p>Hot hand dryers are commonly found in public restrooms and are designed to quickly dry wet hands. There are two main types of hot hand dryers available in the market: hand-in and hand-under. Hand-in dryers require users to insert their hands into a cavity where warm air is blown onto them, while hand-under dryers have users place their hands beneath the unit where the air is blown downwards. Both types serve the same purpose of effectively drying hands, but offer different designs and mechanisms for user convenience.</p></p>
