<p><p>Single-use centrifuge machines are commonly found in two main types - benchtop centrifuges and continuous flow centrifuges. Benchtop centrifuges are small, compact machines that are ideal for smaller labs and research facilities. They are typically used for separating small volumes of samples at high speeds. On the other hand, continuous flow centrifuges are larger machines used for processing larger volumes of samples continuously. They are commonly used in industrial settings where high throughput is required.</p></p>
