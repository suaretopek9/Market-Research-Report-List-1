<p><strong>The Rutile Titanium Dioxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rutile titanium dioxide market is divided into two main types: sulfate process and chloride process. The sulfate process involves treating ilmenite with sulfuric acid to produce titanium dioxide, while the chloride process involves using chlorine and carbon to produce the compound. The sulfate process is more commonly used due to its lower production cost and simpler technology, while the chloride process produces higher-quality titanium dioxide with better optical properties. Both processes cater to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
