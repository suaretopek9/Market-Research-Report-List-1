<p><p>PFPE Pump Fluids market can be categorized into four types: D-type, K-type, Y-type, and Z-type. D-type fluids are low molecular weight perfluoropolyether compounds with good chemical resistance. K-type fluids are medium molecular weight perfluoropolyether compounds with high thermal stability. Y-type fluids are similar to K-type fluids but with enhanced corrosion resistance. Z-type fluids are high molecular weight perfluoropolyether compounds with superior lubricating properties. Each type caters to specific industry requirements and application needs in the PFPE Pump Fluids market.</p></p>
