<p><p>Adhesives for optoelectronics come in three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives are typically used for bonding delicate components together as they provide a strong, yet flexible bond. Solid adhesives, on the other hand, are often used for structural applications, providing a durable and long-lasting bond. Paste adhesives offer a compromise between liquid and solid adhesives, providing the ease of application of a liquid adhesive with the strength and durability of a solid adhesive. Each type of adhesive has its own advantages and applications within the optoelectronics market.</p></p>
