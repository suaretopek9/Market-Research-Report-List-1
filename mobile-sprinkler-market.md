<p><ul><li>Single Track</li><li>Double Track</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile sprinkler market can be divided into two types: single track and double track. In single track market, the mobile sprinkler systems are capable of moving in a straight line only, covering a smaller area efficiently. On the other hand, in double track market, the systems are equipped to move in two parallel tracks, offering a wider coverage area and increased efficiency in watering large fields or gardens. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of customers based on the size of the area to be irrigated.</p></p>
