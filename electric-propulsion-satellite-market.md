<p><ul><li>Gridded Ion Engine (GIE)</li><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric propulsion satellite market types include Gridded Ion Engine (GIE), Hall Effect Thruster (HET), High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT), and Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT). GIE uses a grid to accelerate ions, HET uses electric and magnetic fields to ionize and accelerate propellant, HEMPT utilizes multiple stages for increased efficiency, and PPT generates pulses of plasma for propulsion. These technologies offer varying levels of performance and efficiency to meet the diverse needs of satellite missions.</p></p>
