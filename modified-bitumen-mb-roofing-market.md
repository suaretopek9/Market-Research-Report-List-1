<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen (MB) Roofing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS Type</li><li>APP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Bitumen (MB) Roofing comes in two main types: SBS Type and APP Type. SBS Type MB roofing is made with styrene-butadiene-styrene modifiers, which provide increased flexibility and durability, making it ideal for areas with high foot traffic or extreme weather conditions. On the other hand, APP Type MB roofing uses atactic polypropylene modifiers, which offer improved resistance to UV rays and high temperatures. Both types of MB roofing provide excellent protection and longevity for commercial and residential buildings.</p></p>
