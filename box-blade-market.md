<p><ul><li>Front-tine</li><li>Rear-tine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The box blade market can be broadly categorized into two types: front-tine and rear-tine. Front-tine box blades have their tines positioned at the front of the blade, allowing for more precise control and maneuverability when leveling or grading surfaces. Rear-tine box blades, on the other hand, have their tines positioned at the back, providing more power and stability for heavier-duty tasks. Both types of box blades are commonly used in construction, agriculture, and landscaping for maintaining and shaping terrain.</p></p>
