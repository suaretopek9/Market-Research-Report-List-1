<p><strong>The Reflective Sheeting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bead Type</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective sheeting is available in two main types: Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Glass Bead Type reflective sheeting uses tiny glass beads that reflect light back to its source, providing visibility in low light conditions. On the other hand, Micro Prismatic Type reflective sheeting uses small prisms to reflect light, offering higher visibility from greater distances. Both types are commonly used for road signs, vehicle decals, and safety apparel to improve visibility and prevent accidents in various settings.</p></p>
