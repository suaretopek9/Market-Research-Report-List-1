<p><p>A seamless steel pipe is a type of steel pipe that is manufactured without any welding seam. It is made from a solid round steel billet which is heated and pushed or pulled over a form until the steel is shaped into a hollow tube. This process ensures that the pipe has uniform strength throughout without any weak points that can arise from welding.</p><p>The seamless steel pipe market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period.
