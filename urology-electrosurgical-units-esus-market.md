The market for ESUs includes Monopole units, which use a single active electrode for cutting and coagulation, Bipolar units that use two electrodes for precise tissue sealing, and Vessel Sealing units specifically designed for sealing blood vessels during surgical procedures. Each type of ESU offers unique features and benefits for different urological applications.</p></p>
