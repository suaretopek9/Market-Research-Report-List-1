<p><p>Patient Management Software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing users to access and manage patient data from anywhere with an internet connection. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for onsite infrastructure. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and maintained on local servers, requiring physical presence and IT resources. While it provides greater control and security, it can be more expensive and may necessitate technical expertise for maintenance and upgrades.</p></p>
