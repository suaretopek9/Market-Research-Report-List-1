<p><p>The Electronic Medical Record (EMR) Software market can be divided into two types: On-premise and Cloud Based. On-premise EMR software is installed and hosted on servers within the healthcare provider's facility, requiring maintenance and IT support. On the other hand, cloud-based EMR software operates remotely on the vendor's servers and is accessed through the internet. It offers the advantage of being easily accessible from any location and eliminates the need for on-site hardware maintenance. The choice between these types depends on the healthcare provider's preferences and requirements.</p></p>
