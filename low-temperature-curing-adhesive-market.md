<p><p>Low temperature curing adhesives are available in two main types: waterproof and not waterproof. Waterproof adhesives are designed to withstand exposure to moisture, making them ideal for outdoor or wet environments. On the other hand, non-waterproof adhesives are not designed to resist water and are more suitable for indoor applications where moisture is not a concern. Both types of adhesives offer low temperature curing properties, allowing for efficient bonding in cold environments.</p></p>
