<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Technology has three main market types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF). LF RFID is commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF RFID is utilized for payment systems and smart cards. UHF RFID is popular in supply chain management and inventory tracking due to its longer read range. Each market type caters to specific applications and industries, offering various benefits such as improved efficiency, accuracy, and security.</p></p>
