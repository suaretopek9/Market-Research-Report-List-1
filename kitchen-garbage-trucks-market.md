<p><p>Kitchen garbage trucks come in three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders have a large opening at the front of the truck for dumping waste into, making them ideal for centralized locations. Rear loaders have a hydraulic packer that compacts the waste at the back of the truck, making them suitable for residential areas. Side loaders have an opening on the side of the truck, allowing for easy collection in narrow streets or alleys. Each type caters to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
