<p><strong>The Mounted Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-point Hitch</li><li>ATV-mounted</li><li>Front-mount</li><li>Straddle Tractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mounted sprayer market consists of various types based on how the sprayer is attached to the vehicle or equipment. The 3-point hitch sprayers are commonly used with tractors, providing stability and ease of maneuverability. ATV-mounted sprayers are designed for use with all-terrain vehicles, offering flexibility in navigating rough terrain. Front-mount sprayers are mounted on the front of the vehicle, allowing for precise application in front of the operator. Straddle tractor sprayers are designed to be mounted on both sides of the tractor, providing efficient coverage in large fields.</p></p>
