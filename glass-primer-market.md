<p><ul><li>Silane Glass Primer</li><li>Urethane Glass Primer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silane Glass Primer is a type of glass primer that uses silane molecules to create a strong bond between the glass surface and the coating applied on top. It is known for its excellent adhesion properties and durability, making it suitable for various applications. Urethane Glass Primer, on the other hand, utilizes urethane molecules to achieve similar results. It offers good chemical resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for use in environments where the glass surface may be exposed to harsh conditions. Both types of glass primers are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
