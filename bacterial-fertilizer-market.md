<p><p>Bacterial fertilizer market includes different types of products based on their mechanisms of action. Nitrogen-fixing bacterial fertilizers contain bacteria that can convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form that plants can use for growth. Phosphate-solubilizing bacterial fertilizers contain bacteria that can break down insoluble phosphorus compounds in the soil, making it available to plants. Other types of bacterial fertilizers may include those that help with nutrient uptake, disease prevention, or soil health improvement. These products offer various benefits for sustainable and effective agriculture.</p></p>
