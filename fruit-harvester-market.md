<p><p>Fruit harvesters come in three main types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted. Self-propelled harvesters are machines that can move on their own and are typically used for large-scale commercial orchards. Trailed harvesters are pulled behind a tractor and are suitable for medium-sized orchards. Mounted harvesters are attached to the front or back of a tractor and are used for smaller orchards. Each type of harvester serves a specific market segment based on the size and needs of the orchard.</p></p>
