<p><p>Electric motorcycle tires are typically divided into two main market types - OEM and aftermarket. OEM tires are those that are supplied by the original equipment manufacturer of the motorcycle and are typically sold as part of the complete motorcycle unit. Aftermarket tires, on the other hand, are produced by third-party manufacturers and can be purchased separately from the motorcycle. While OEM tires are often seen as higher quality and more reliable, aftermarket tires offer a wider range of options and customization for riders.</p></p>
