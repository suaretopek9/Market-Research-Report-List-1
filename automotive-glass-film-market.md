<p><ul><li>Tinted Film</li><li>Metal Film</li><li>Sputtering Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive glass film market consists of various types such as tinted film, metal film, and sputtering film. Tinted film is designed to reduce glare and improve privacy by darkening the glass. Metal film provides enhanced heat rejection and UV protection. Sputtering film involves applying a thin layer of metal or metal oxide onto the glass surface to achieve properties like heat insulation and scratch resistance. Each type of film offers unique benefits and caters to different needs of consumers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
