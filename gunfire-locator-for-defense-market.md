<p><p>Gunfire locator systems for defense can be categorized into two main types: acoustic and optical. Acoustic systems use sensitive sensors to detect and locate gunshots based on sound waves, providing real-time data to military personnel for rapid response. Optical systems, on the other hand, utilize advanced imaging technology to pinpoint the origin of gunfire by analyzing visual cues such as muzzle flashes. Both types of systems play a crucial role in enhancing situational awareness and improving overall security in defense settings.</p></p>
