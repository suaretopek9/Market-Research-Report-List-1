<p><p>There are three main types of radiator coolants in the market: silicate-based, organic salt-based, and others. Silicate-based coolants contain silicate inhibitors to protect against corrosion and rust in the cooling system. Organic salt-based coolants use organic acid technology to provide long-lasting protection against rust and corrosion. The "others" category includes different types of coolants such as phosphate-based and nitrite-based options. Each type offers unique benefits and is designed for specific applications in different vehicles.</p></p>
