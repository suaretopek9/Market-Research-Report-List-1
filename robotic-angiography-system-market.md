<p><p>Robotic Angiography Systems are used for medical imaging of blood vessels to diagnose various conditions. There are three main types in the market: CT Angiography Systems, X-ray Angiography Systems, and MR Angiography Systems. CT Angiography Systems use computed tomography technology to produce detailed images of blood vessels. X-ray Angiography Systems use X-rays to visualize blood vessels in real time. MR Angiography Systems use magnetic resonance imaging to create detailed images of blood vessels without the use of ionizing radiation. Each system has its own advantages and applications in healthcare.</p></p>
