<p><p>Polyester forming fabrics are commonly classified based on the number of sheds and layers they possess. 4-Shed Single Layer fabrics offer good drainage and fiber support. 5-Shed Single Layer fabrics provide even drainage and excellent fiber support. 8-Shed Single Layer fabrics offer high dewatering performance. 8-Shed Double Layer fabrics have increased stability and improved fiber retention. 16-Shed Double and A Half Layer fabrics offer high-speed papermaking capabilities. SSB Forming Fabrics are known for their high durability and abrasion resistance, making them ideal for demanding papermaking applications.</p></p>
