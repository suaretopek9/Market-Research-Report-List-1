<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Converter Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Back-To-Back</li><li>Bipolar</li><li>Multi-Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Converter transformer market consists of various types based on the connections and configurations of the transformers. Monopolar converter transformers are used in high voltage direct current (HVDC) transmission systems with only one converter per pole. Back-to-back converter transformers are used in HVDC systems to connect two asynchronous grids. Bipolar converter transformers are used in HVDC systems with two converters per pole. Multi-terminal converter transformers are used in HVDC systems with multiple terminals for connecting multiple grids or points of power injection. Each type serves a specific purpose in HVDC transmission systems.</p></p>
