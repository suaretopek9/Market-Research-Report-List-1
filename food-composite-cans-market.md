<p><p>Food composite cans are available in three main types based on the material used: cardboard, plastic, and aluminum foil. Cardboard material cans are cost-effective and environmentally friendly, making them a popular choice among consumers. Plastic material cans offer durability and resistance to moisture, making them suitable for a wide range of food products. Aluminum foil material cans provide excellent barrier protection against light, oxygen, and moisture, ensuring the freshness and quality of the food for a longer period.</p></p>
