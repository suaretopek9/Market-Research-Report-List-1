<p><p>Electronic Circuit Board Level Underfill Material are used in applications such as CSP (Chip Scale Package), BGA (Ball Grid Array), and Flip Chips to provide mechanical support and protection to the circuit components. CSPs are compact integrated circuits with external connections on the same scale as the chip size. BGAs are packages with solder balls on the underside for high-density mounting. Flip Chips are chips with active circuitry facing down and connected to the substrate. Underfill materials help improve the reliability and durability of these electronic components.</p></p>
