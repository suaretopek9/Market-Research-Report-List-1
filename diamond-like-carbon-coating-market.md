<p><p>Diamond-Like Carbon (DLC) coating is a type of protective coating used in various industries. There are two main types of DLC coatings: Hydrogen Free DLC and Hydrogenated DLC. Hydrogen Free DLC is a type of DLC coating that does not contain hydrogen, making it ideal for applications where hydrogen may cause issues. On the other hand, Hydrogenated DLC contains hydrogen and is often used in applications where flexibility and wear resistance are paramount. Both types of DLC coatings offer excellent protection against wear, corrosion, and abrasion.</p></p>
