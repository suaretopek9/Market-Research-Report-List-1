<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Timer Controlled Drain market includes two main types: High Pressure and Low Pressure. High Pressure timer controlled drains are designed to efficiently remove condensate from high pressure systems, such as air compressors and pneumatic tools. These drains can handle the higher flow rates and pressures associated with these systems. On the other hand, Low Pressure timer controlled drains are specifically designed for low pressure systems, such as refrigeration units and air dryers. They are capable of effectively draining condensate from these systems at lower pressures and flow rates.</p></p>
