<p><p>Tumor ablation market types refer to different technologies used for destroying tumors using minimal invasive procedures. Radiofrequency (RF) Ablation involves the use of electrical currents to heat and destroy the tumor cells. Microwave Ablation uses microwave energy to generate heat and kill the tumor tissue. Cryoablation uses extreme cold temperatures to freeze and destroy the tumor cells. Other Technologies include High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) and Laser Ablation, which use sound waves and laser energy respectively to ablate tumors. These techniques aim to eliminate cancer cells without the need for surgery.</p></p>
