<p><p>The Airport Information System market consists of various types, including Airport Operation Control Centers (AOCC) and Departure Control Systems (DCS). AOCCs are centralized platforms that allow for real-time monitoring and management of airport activities, such as flight scheduling, ground handling, and resource allocation. DCSs, on the other hand, are responsible for passenger check-in, boarding, and baggage handling processes. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring efficient airport operations and enhancing the overall passenger experience.</p></p>
