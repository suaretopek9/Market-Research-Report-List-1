<p><p>The flower seeds market is categorized into three main types: annual seeds, biennial seeds, and perennial seeds. Annual seeds produce flowers in the same year they are planted and need to be replanted each year. Biennial seeds take two years to produce flowers, with the plant typically flowering in the second year. Perennial seeds produce flowers year after year without needing to be replanted. Each type caters to different preferences and gardening needs, providing a variety of options for consumers in the flower seeds market.</p></p>
