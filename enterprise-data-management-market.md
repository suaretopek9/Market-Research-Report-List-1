<p><p>Enterprise data management (EDM) market consists of two primary types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise EDM involves the deployment of data management solutions within an organization's own infrastructure, providing complete control over data security and customization. In contrast, Cloud-based EDM utilizes remote servers and internet connectivity to offer scalable and flexible data management solutions. Cloud-based EDM offers the advantage of reduced upfront costs, ease of maintenance, and the ability to access data from anywhere, whereas On-Premise provides enhanced security, compliance, and customization options.</p></p>
