<p><p>Surface Mount Technology (SMT) Equipment market includes three main types: Placement Equipment, Printer Equipment, and Reflow Oven Equipment. Placement Equipment is used for accurately placing electronic components onto PCBs. Printer Equipment is used for applying solder paste onto PCBs before components are placed. Reflow Oven Equipment is used for heating the solder paste to melt and create connections between components and the PCB. These three types of equipment are vital in the SMT process to ensure high-quality and efficient assembly of electronic devices.</p></p>
