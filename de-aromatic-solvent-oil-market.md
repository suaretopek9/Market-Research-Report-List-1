<p><p>De-aromatic solvent oil is categorized into three main types based on their flash points: low flash point, medium flash point, and high flash point. Low flash point de-aromatic solvent oils have a flash point below 100 degrees Celsius, making them highly flammable. Medium flash point oils have a flash point between 100 and 139 degrees Celsius, offering a balance between flammability and volatility. High flash point oils have a flash point above 139 degrees Celsius, making them less flammable and more suitable for applications requiring higher safety standards.</p></p>
