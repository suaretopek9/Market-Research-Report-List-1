<p><ul><li>Cement Grouting Machine</li><li>Chemical Grouting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grouting machines are commonly divided into two types: Cement Grouting Machines and Chemical Grouting Machines. Cement Grouting Machines are used for injecting cement-based grout into gaps and crevices to fill voids and strengthen structures. On the other hand, Chemical Grouting Machines are utilized for injecting chemical grouts like polyurethane or epoxy resins to seal leaks, stabilize soil, or repair cracks in concrete structures. Both types of machines play a crucial role in construction, infrastructure maintenance, and repair projects.</p></p>
