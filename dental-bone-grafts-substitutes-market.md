<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li><li>Synthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental bone grafts and substitutes market types include allografts, xenografts, and synthetic materials. Allografts are bone grafts taken from another human donor, xenografts are derived from animals, typically bovine or porcine sources, while synthetic materials are man-made substitutes. Allografts are popular due to their ability to promote bone regeneration, xenografts are advantageous for their structural similarities to human bone, and synthetic materials offer a consistent and customizable option. Each type has unique benefits and is used based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
