<p><p>The construction industry core drill market is segmented into two main types: wet drill bits and dry drill bits. Wet drill bits are used with water to cool and lubricate the cutting edge during drilling, reducing heat and extending bit life. Dry drill bits do not require water and are used for drilling into hardened materials such as concrete and masonry. Both types of drill bits cater to specific drilling needs and are essential tools in the construction industry for creating holes in various surfaces.</p></p>
