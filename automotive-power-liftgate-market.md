<p><p>The Automotive Power Liftgate market includes two main types: Conventional Power Liftgate and Hands-free Power Liftgate. Conventional Power Liftgate systems require the user to physically press a button or use a key fob to open and close the liftgate. On the other hand, Hands-free Power Liftgate systems allow users to open and close the liftgate without the need to physically touch or interact with the vehicle, using sensors and smart technology to detect the user's presence and automatically operate the liftgate.</p></p>
