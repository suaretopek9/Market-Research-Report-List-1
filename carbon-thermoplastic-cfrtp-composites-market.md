<p><ul><li>PAN</li><li>Pitch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites are classified into two main types based on the precursor material used for the carbon fibers: PAN-based and Pitch-based composites. PAN-based composites are derived from polyacrylonitrile fibers and are known for their high strength, stiffness, and excellent fatigue resistance. On the other hand, Pitch-based composites are produced from petroleum or coal tar pitch and are valued for their superior thermal and electrical conductivity properties. Both types of CFRTP composites offer unique advantages for various applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
