<p><p>The Powerline Carrier market can be divided into two types: Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. Narrowband PLC refers to a system that operates at a low frequency range, typically lower than 500 kHz. It is commonly used for applications such as smart metering and home automation. On the other hand, Broadband PLC operates at a higher frequency range, often above 1 MHz, enabling faster data transmission rates. Broadband PLC is widely used for applications like internet access and multimedia streaming. Both types offer efficient communication through existing power lines, but with different capabilities and applications.</p></p>
