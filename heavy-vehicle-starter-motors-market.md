<p><strong>The Heavy Vehicle Starter Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial (Sliding Armature)</li><li>Coaxial (Sliding Gear)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heavy vehicle starter motors can be classified into two main types: Axial (Sliding Armature) and Coaxial (Sliding Gear) starters. Axial starters have a moving armature that slides along the axis of rotation, while coaxial starters have a sliding gear mechanism. Axial starters are commonly used in larger vehicles due to their higher torque capabilities, while coaxial starters are preferred for smaller vehicles due to their compact size. Both types of starter motors play a crucial role in providing the necessary power to start heavy vehicles.</p></p>
