<p><p>The air conditioning installation market can be categorized into two main types: whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems are designed to cool or heat an entire residential or commercial building using a centralized unit that distributes air through ductwork. On the other hand, split systems consist of an outdoor unit that houses the condenser and an indoor unit that contains the evaporator. They are often used for cooling or heating individual rooms or smaller spaces. Both types cater to different cooling needs and preferences.</p></p>
