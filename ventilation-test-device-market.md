<p><p>Ventilation test devices are used to measure and analyze airflow in various settings. Air velocity meters or anemometers are instruments used to measure the speed and direction of airflow. Manometers or micromanometers are devices that measure pressure differentials to ensure proper ventilation. Leakage testers are used to detect leaks in ventilation systems. Air capture hoods are devices used to measure air volume in HVAC systems. Each type of device plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient and effective ventilation systems in buildings.</p></p>
