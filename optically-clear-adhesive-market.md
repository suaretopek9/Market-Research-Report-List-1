<p><ul><li>Resistance Type</li><li>Capacitance Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optically Clear Adhesive (OCA) comes in two main types - Resistance Type and Capacitance Type. Resistance Type OCAs are used in applications where the pressure-sensitive adhesive is required to provide a conductive path between two surfaces. Capacitance Type OCAs, on the other hand, are used in touch screens and other applications where the adhesive must maintain a specific capacitance. Both types offer high transparency and allow for optical bonding without compromising the quality of display screens.</p></p>
