<p><p>Gun scopes are divided into two main types on the market: optical and mechanical scopes. Optical scopes rely on lenses to magnify the target and provide clear images. They are popular for their accuracy and precision. On the other hand, mechanical scopes use internal mechanisms to adjust elevation and windage for better targeting. Both types offer unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences of shooters. The choice between optical and mechanical scopes ultimately depends on individual needs and shooting style.</p></p>
