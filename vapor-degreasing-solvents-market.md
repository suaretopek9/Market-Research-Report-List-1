<p><p>Vapor Degreasing Solvents market types include Npb, TCE, Perc, and Others. Npb (n-propyl bromide) is commonly used as a replacement for traditional chlorinated solvents due to its low toxicity and environmental impact. TCE (trichloroethylene) is a powerful solvent with excellent degreasing properties, but it is considered carcinogenic and has been restricted in some regions. Perc (perchloroethylene) is another commonly used degreasing solvent that is effective but poses health risks. Other solvents in this market vary in their composition, toxicity, and environmental impact.</p></p>
