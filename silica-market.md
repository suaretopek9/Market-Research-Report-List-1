<p><p>Silica market is divided into three main types - Quartz, Tridymite, and Cristobalite. Quartz is the most commonly found form of silica and is used in various industries such as construction and electronics. Tridymite is a high-temperature polymorph of silica, often used in ceramics and refractories. Cristobalite is another high-temperature form of silica, mainly used in the production of ceramics and glass. Each type of silica offers unique properties and is utilized in different applications based on their characteristics.</p></p>
