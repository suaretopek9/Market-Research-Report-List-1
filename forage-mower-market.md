<p><p>Forage mowers can be classified into different types based on their mechanism of operation. Rotary mowers have blades that rotate horizontally, cutting the forage efficiently. Rotating disc-type mowers have disc blades that rotate vertically, offering a precise cutting action. Cylinder type mowers have a cylindrical blade that moves back and forth to cut the forage. Other types of forage mowers may include flail mowers or sickle mowers, each offering unique benefits depending on the user's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
