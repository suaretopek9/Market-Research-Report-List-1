<p><p>Differential pressure switches are instruments used to measure and control the difference in pressure between two points. They consist of a sensing element, usually a diaphragm or bellows, and a switch mechanism. When the difference in pressure exceeds a set threshold, the switch triggers an electrical circuit to open or close, depending on the application requirements.</p><p>The market for differential pressure switches is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for these switches in various industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and water & wastewater is driving market growth. Differential pressure switches are widely used in these sectors for applications like filter monitoring, level detection, and flow measurement.</p><p>Additionally, the growing focus on energy efficiency and sustainability is also influencing the market growth. Differential pressure switches are essential in HVAC systems and air handling units to maintain optimal airflow and pressure levels, ensuring energy efficiency. With the increasing adoption of energy-efficient technologies, the demand for these switches is expected to rise.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements and innovations in sensor technology are contributing to market growth. These smart switches offer enhanced accuracy and reliability, making them attractive for various industries.</p><p>Overall, the differential pressure switches market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand from various industries and the emphasis on energy efficiency.
