<p><p>Internal mold releases are categorized into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I internal mold releases are water-based products that do not contain any harmful solvents. They are largely used in applications that require eco-friendly and safe release agents. On the other hand, Type II internal mold releases are solvent-based products that offer higher performance in terms of release efficiency and durability. They are commonly used in industrial settings where a more robust release agent is needed.</p></p>
