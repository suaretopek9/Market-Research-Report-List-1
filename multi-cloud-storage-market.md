<p><p>The multi-cloud storage market encompasses different types of cloud storage deployments - public, private, and hybrid. Public cloud storage refers to services offered by providers to the general public over the internet. Private cloud storage is dedicated to a single organization, offering enhanced security and control. Hybrid cloud storage combines both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to store sensitive data in a private cloud and leverage the scalability and cost-effectiveness of a public cloud for less critical data. These different market types offer organizations flexibility in choosing the storage solutions that best suit their needs.</p></p>
