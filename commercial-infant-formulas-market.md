<p><p>Commercial infant formulas are classified into various types based on their composition. Cow milk protein-based formulas are the most common, suitable for most infants. Soy-based formulas are designed for infants with cow milk protein allergies. Protein hydrolysate formulas are broken down into smaller proteins for easier digestion, ideal for infants with digestive issues. Other types include specialized formulas for premature infants or those with specific medical conditions. Each type serves a specific purpose in providing essential nutrients for the healthy development of infants.</p></p>
