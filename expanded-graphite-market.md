<p><p>Expanded Graphite market can be categorized into three main types based on the form in which it is available: Packing Form, Gasket Form, and Weave Form. Packing Form refers to expanded graphite in the form of strands or ropes used for sealing applications. Gasket Form involves expanded graphite sheets used in making gaskets for industrial equipment. Weave Form includes expanded graphite fabric used for various high-temperature applications. Each type serves a specific purpose in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
