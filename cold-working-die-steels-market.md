<p><strong>The Cold Working Die Steels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-hardening Cold-work Steels</li><li>Air-hardening Cold-work Steels</li><li>High carbon-chromium Cold-work Steels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold working die steels are divided into three main types based on their hardening process:</p><p>1. Oil-hardening cold-work steels are typically used for applications requiring high wear resistance and good toughness. These steels are quenched in oil to achieve the desired hardness level.</p><p>2. Air-hardening cold-work steels are known for their high hardenability and wear resistance. These steels are quenched in air to achieve the required hardness level.</p><p>3. High carbon-chromium cold-work steels offer superior wear resistance and hardenability due to their high carbon and chromium content. These steels are suitable for high-stress applications that require excellent cutting performance.</p></p>
