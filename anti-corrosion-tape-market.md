<p><p>Anti-corrosion tape is used to protect pipelines and other metal surfaces from corrosion. The market is categorized into three main types: petrolatum-based, polymer-based, and others. Petrolatum-based tapes are made from a wax-like substance that forms a protective barrier against moisture and chemicals. Polymer-based tapes are made from polymer resins that provide a durable and long-lasting protection. Other types of anti-corrosion tapes may include adhesive or bitumen-based products. Each type of tape offers unique properties and benefits for specific applications in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
