Edge computing allows processing and analysis of data at the edge of the network, closer to the devices generating the data. This approach reduces latency, improves bandwidth utilization, and enhances real-time decision-making capabilities.</p><p>Furthermore, the demand for secure and scalable IIoT platforms is increasing. Organizations are looking for platforms that provide robust security features to protect their sensitive data and operations from cyber threats.
