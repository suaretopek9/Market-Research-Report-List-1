<p><ul><li>Front Crash Management</li><li>Rear Crash Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crash Management Systems market includes various types such as Front Crash Management and Rear Crash Management. Front Crash Management focuses on reducing the impact and severity of collisions from the front, utilizing technologies like automatic emergency braking and forward collision warning. On the other hand, Rear Crash Management aims to mitigate the consequences of rear-end collisions by implementing features like rear automatic braking and blind spot detection. Both systems work towards enhancing safety and preventing accidents, but for different types of crash scenarios.</p></p>
