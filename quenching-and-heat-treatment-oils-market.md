<p><ul><li>Cold Oils</li><li>Semi-hot Oils</li><li>Hot Oils</li><li>Bright Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quenching and heat treatment oils are essential in the metalworking industry to control the rate of cooling and improve the properties of metal parts. Cold oils offer a slow cooling rate, ideal for reducing distortion and cracking. Semi-hot oils provide moderate cooling rates for uniform hardening. Hot oils offer rapid cooling for high hardenability steels. Bright oils are designed for specialized applications requiring a clean, bright surface finish. Each type of oil caters to specific needs and requirements in the heat treatment process.</p></p>
