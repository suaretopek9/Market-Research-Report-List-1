<p><p>Electric Vehicle (EV) driveline fluids market includes synthetic oil and mineral oil types. Synthetic oil is a high-performance lubricant formulated for better protection and efficiency in EV driveline systems. It offers superior thermal stability, oxidation resistance, and longevity compared to mineral oil. On the other hand, mineral oil is a more traditional lubricant option for EV drivelines, offering basic protection and lubrication. The choice between synthetic and mineral oil depends on cost, performance requirements, and environmental concerns.</p></p>
