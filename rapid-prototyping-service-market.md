<p><strong>The Rapid Prototyping Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SLS</li><li>SLA</li><li>FDM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid prototyping services encompass various market types, including Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Stereolithography (SLA), Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), and others. SLS employs a laser to sinter powdered material into a solid model layer by layer. SLA uses a UV laser to solidify liquid resin, also layer by layer, creating a 3D model. FDM builds objects by extruding melted material through a nozzle, subsequently hardening it layer by layer. Other market types include Polyjet, Digital Light Processing (DLP), and Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM), each with different methods of layering and forming prototypes.</p></p>
