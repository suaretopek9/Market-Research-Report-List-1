<p><p>The recycled pulp market can be divided into three main types: Mechanical pulping, Chemical pulping, and Others. Mechanical pulping involves grinding wood fibers to separate them from each other. Chemical pulping uses chemicals to break down the wood into pulp. Other methods include de-inking processes and secondary fiber pulping. Each type of pulping method has its advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, environmental impact, and end product quality. The choice of method depends on factors such as the type of paper being produced and the desired characteristics of the final product.</p></p>
