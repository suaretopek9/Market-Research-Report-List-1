<p><ul><li>Rubber Bellows Type</li><li>Sleeve-Style Type</li><li>Coil Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspension air bags have three main types in the market: rubber bellows type, sleeve-style type, and coil type. Rubber bellows type air bags are made of flexible rubber material that compresses and expands to adjust the suspension. Sleeve-style air bags have a reinforced sleeve that provides durability and stability. Coil type air bags use a metal coil spring to support the weight of the vehicle. Each type of suspension air bag offers unique benefits and features to cater to different vehicle suspension needs.</p></p>
