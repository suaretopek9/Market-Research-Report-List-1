<p><p>The Polysilicon Rod Market can be categorized into two main types: Silane Method and Metallurgical Process. </p><p>The Silane Method involves the use of silane gas to deposit silicon onto a rod, which results in high purity polysilicon suitable for electronic applications. </p><p>On the other hand, the Metallurgical Process involves the reduction of silicon tetrachloride using a high temperature furnace, producing lower purity polysilicon primarily used in the production of solar panels.</p><p>Both methods have their own advantages and applications within the polysilicon industry.</p></p>
