PLA is derived from corn starch and sugarcane, offering a biodegradable alternative to traditional plastics. Starch blends combine starch with other polymers to improve biodegradability. PBAT and PBS are biodegradable copolymers with properties suitable for packaging and other applications. Other types of bioplastics include Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA) and Polyethylene Glycol (PEG).</p></p>
