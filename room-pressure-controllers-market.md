<p><p>Room pressure controllers are devices that are used to maintain a desired pressure difference between different areas or rooms within a building. They are primarily employed in healthcare facilities, cleanrooms, laboratories, manufacturing plants, and other environments where it is crucial to control the air pressure in order to maintain a sterile or safe environment.</p><p>These controllers typically consist of a sensor that measures the pressure, a controller that compares the actual pressure to the desired set point, and a control valve or damper that adjusts the airflow to maintain the desired pressure. The main function of room pressure controllers is to prevent the cross-contamination of air between different areas by ensuring that air always flows from a higher pressure area to a lower pressure area.</p><p>The room pressure controllers market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors such as increasing demand for healthcare facilities, rising adoption of cleanrooms in various industries, and the implementation of stringent regulations and guidelines for air quality control are driving the market growth.
