<p><strong>The Hydrogenated Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5</li><li>C9</li><li>DCPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogenated Resin market includes three main types: C5, C9, and DCPD. C5 hydrogenated resin is derived from C5 hydrocarbons and is commonly used in adhesives, coatings, and rubber compounding. C9 hydrogenated resin, sourced from C9 aromatic hydrocarbons, finds applications in hot-melt adhesives, paints, and printing inks. DCPD hydrogenated resin, obtained from dicyclopentadiene, is utilized in producing tackifiers for adhesives and sealants. Each type offers unique properties and benefits, catering to different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
