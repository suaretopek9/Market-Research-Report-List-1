<p><p>Soft magnetic powder core market consists of various types such as MPP, Sendust, High Flux, Fe-Si, and others. MPP cores offer high performance and low losses at high frequencies. Sendust cores provide good saturation flux density and are cost-effective. High Flux cores offer high magnetic induction and superior thermal stability. Fe-Si cores are known for high permeability and low core losses. Other types of cores include iron powder cores and ferrite cores, each offering unique advantages in different applications.</p></p>
