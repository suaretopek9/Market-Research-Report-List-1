<p><p>The sanding accessories market can be categorized into two types: bonded and coated. Bonded sanding accessories consist of abrasive grains bonded together with resin, providing durability and control during sanding tasks. On the other hand, coated sanding accessories are coated with abrasive materials such as sandpaper or emery cloth, allowing for a smoother finish and precision in sanding applications. Both types of sanding accessories play a crucial role in various industries, including woodworking, metalworking, and automotive.</p></p>
