<p><strong>The Automobile Water Pump Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Roller Bearings</li><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile water pump bearings come in two main types: cylindrical roller bearings and deep groove ball bearings. Cylindrical roller bearings are designed to handle heavy radial loads and are commonly used in water pump systems requiring high rotational speeds. On the other hand, deep groove ball bearings have a simple design, allowing them to handle both radial and axial loads. These bearings are often used in water pump systems with moderate rotational speeds and where space is limited.</p></p>
