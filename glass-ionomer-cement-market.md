<p><p>Glass-ionomer cement is a dental material that is commonly used for restorative and orthodontic purposes. It is a blend of an organic acid and glass powder, forming a hard and translucent material when set. Glass-ionomer cement has various applications in dentistry, including cavity fillings, cementing crowns or bridges, and as a lining or base under other restorative materials.</p><p>The glass-ionomer cement market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of dental diseases, rising dental tourism, and growing awareness regarding dental aesthetics are some of the key factors driving market growth. Additionally, the shift towards minimally invasive dental procedures and the development of advanced glass-ionomer cement formulations with improved physical and mechanical properties are further contributing to market expansion.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the glass-ionomer cement market is the incorporation of bioactive components into the cement formulation. These bioactive materials release fluoride, calcium, and phosphate ions, which promote remineralization of tooth structure and reduce the risk of secondary tooth decay. Such advancements are driving the adoption of bioactive glass-ionomer cements, especially in pediatric dentistry.</p><p>Furthermore, manufacturers are focusing on product innovations and introducing new formulations with enhanced bonding properties and improved esthetics. These developments are expected to boost the demand for glass-ionomer cement in the coming years.</p><p>Overall, with the growing demand for dental restorative materials and the increasing preference for minimally invasive dental procedures, the glass-ionomer cement market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.</p></p>
