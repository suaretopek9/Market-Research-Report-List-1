<p><p>The herb harvester market can be divided into two main types based on their mode of operation: self-propelled herb harvesters and mounted herb harvesters. Self-propelled herb harvesters are equipped with their own engine and can move independently in the field, making them ideal for larger-scale operations. On the other hand, mounted herb harvesters are attached to tractors or other vehicles for mobility, making them more suitable for smaller farms or limited harvesting areas. Both types of herb harvesters are used for efficiently collecting herbs in agriculture.</p></p>
