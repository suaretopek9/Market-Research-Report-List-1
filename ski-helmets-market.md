<p><p>Ski helmets are available in two main types: PC Shell and ABS Shell. PC Shell helmets are made from polycarbonate, a durable yet lightweight material that provides excellent impact protection. ABS Shell helmets are made from acrylonitrile butadiene styrene, which is also strong and impact-resistant. PC Shell helmets are typically more expensive due to their higher level of protection and quality, while ABS Shell helmets are more budget-friendly options. Both types provide necessary head protection for skiers on the slopes.</p></p>
