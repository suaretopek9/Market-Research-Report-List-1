<p><p>Lower Limb Exoskeleton Robots can be classified based on power supply into two main types: powered and non-powered. </p><p>Powered exoskeleton robots are equipped with motors and actuators that assist in the movement of the wearer's lower limbs. These robots are commonly used in rehabilitation settings to support individuals with limited mobility.</p><p>Non-powered exoskeleton robots rely on mechanical design and passive structures to provide support and assistance to the wearer.
