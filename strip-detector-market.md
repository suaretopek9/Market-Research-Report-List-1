<p><p>The strip detector market encompasses various types of detectors, including silicon strip detectors and 3D CZT drift strip detectors. Silicon strip detectors are widely used due to their excellent radiation hardness, fast response, and high spatial resolution. On the other hand, 3D CZT drift strip detectors utilize cadmium zinc telluride crystals and offer superior energy resolution and sensitivity to X-rays and gamma rays. Both types of detectors find applications in fields like particle physics, medical imaging, nuclear energy, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
