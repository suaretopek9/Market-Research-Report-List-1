<p><p>The aluminum food can market consists of two main types: the three-piece can and the two-piece can. A three-piece can is made up of three components - a bottom, body, and top lid. This type of can is typically used for products that require airtight sealing, such as infant formula or coffee. On the other hand, a two-piece can is formed from a single piece of aluminum, eliminating the need for a separate lid. This type of can is commonly used for beverages and other non-perishable food items.</p></p>
