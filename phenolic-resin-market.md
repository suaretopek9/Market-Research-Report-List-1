<p><strong>The Phenolic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resol Resin</li><li>Novolac Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phenolic resin market is divided into Resol Resin and Novolac Resin types. Resol Resin is a type of phenolic resin that is produced by reacting phenol with formaldehyde in the presence of an alkaline catalyst. It is known for its excellent heat resistance and high mechanical strength. Novolac Resin, on the other hand, is produced by reacting phenol with an excess of formaldehyde in the presence of an acidic catalyst. It is valued for its exceptional chemical resistance and superior dimensional stability.</p></p>
